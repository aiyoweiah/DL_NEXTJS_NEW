A warm, slow read of Syd Hoff's classic — recorded with care for young
listeners learning English. Narrated by **Miss Jennifer** with a guest
character voice for the dinosaur.

## Level

**A2 (Elementary)** with one or two B1 stretch words. Suitable for ages
5–8, or older learners building listening comfort in English.

## Vocabulary preview

Before the story, Miss Jennifer introduces five words slowly, each with a
short definition and a sample sentence:

- **camp** — a place outside where children stay and play together
- **ketchup** — a sweet red sauce made from tomatoes
- **hike** — a long walk, usually in nature
- **marshmallows** — soft, sweet candies that get warm and gooey by a fire
- **bunk** — a small bed, often used at camp

## How to listen

Press play and follow along. The narration is paced for ESL listeners
(0.78× of normal reading speed for the story body, 0.85× for the host
sections), with longer pauses between scenes so children have time to
picture what's happening.
