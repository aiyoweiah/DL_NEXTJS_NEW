Eight-year-old Jack and his impulsive little sister Annie discover the highest tree house in the world, hidden deep in the woods near their home in Frog Creek, Pennsylvania.
The tree house is filled with books.
When Annie picks one up, points at the pictures, and makes a wish, the tree house starts to spin…
Where will it take them, and how will they find their way home?
